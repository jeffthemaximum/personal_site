About Me

I'm a freelance web developer, and Recurse Center alum. I'm Director of Technology at Inwood Academy for Leadership Charter School. I've also taught for nine years in K-12 schools, and currently teach CS50AP to NYC high school students.

Big Things I've Made

  • Crispy Hippopotamus: A browser based graphical user interface for xboard compatible chess engines, such as gnuchess. Lets you play against some of the world's best chess engines in your browser. Source code here.
  • Jeffit: A Reddit clone written in Ruby on Rails that I used in my 6th grade Social Studies classroom. Students posted projects to Jeffit, then voted and commented on others' work. Source code here.
